Applications are now open for the ninth cohort of TRI AI Saturdays Lagos, our flagship community-led learning programme.

Cohort 9 will run for twelve weeks beginning in April. As with previous cohorts, the programme is free, structured around weekly meet-ups in Lagos, and combines self-paced study with peer learning and mentorship from working AI practitioners.

We particularly encourage applications from final-year students, recent graduates, and self-taught engineers in Nigeria who want a structured route into machine learning and applied AI.

The application form and detail are on the Programmes page.
