TRI AI researchers have released ChowNet, an image dataset and benchmark for recognising African cuisine.

Existing food-recognition datasets contain little African food imagery, leaving downstream applications - from nutrition tools to inventory systems - poorly suited to billions of meals eaten across the continent each year. ChowNet is a first step at addressing that imbalance, with paired baselines on contemporary vision models and a clear path forward for community contributions.

The dataset, code, and accompanying paper are linked from the Publications page. We welcome contributions of additional imagery, annotations, and evaluation tasks.
