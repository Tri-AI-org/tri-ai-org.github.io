*May 2026*
 
Cohort 10 of **TRI AI Saturdays** kicks off on **31 May 2026**, running for 16 weeks and powered by the **Google DeepMind AI Research Foundations** curriculum. The cohort is organised by **TRI-AI (Artificial Intelligence Teaching, Research and Innovation for Africa)**.
 
The curriculum behind the cohort was developed by **Google DeepMind in partnership with University College London (UCL)** and launched globally on 21 October 2025. While the courses are open worldwide, they were deliberately localised for educators and learners in Africa, where access to advanced AI education has historically been limited. Cohort 10 runs Courses 1 through 4 of that curriculum.
 
> "For years, educators in Africa have relied on free and open courses to teach machine learning and AI, because creating high-quality content from scratch takes resources many communities like ours don't have. That's why efforts like this matter. They make it possible for us to tailor world-class learning materials to local contexts, helping more people understand the fundamentals of cutting-edge AI models, so they can meaningfully contribute to AI."
> 
> **Tejumade Afonja**, PhD Researcher at CISPA, Director of TRI-AI, and Co-founder of AI Saturdays Lagos
 
### What participants will do
 
* Learn the core concepts powering modern language models: **tokenization, embeddings, backpropagation, gradient descent, and attention mechanisms**
* Translate the mathematical formulation of the attention head into code, and implement **multi-head attention** from the ground up
* Build a working **Small Language Model (SLM)** as a hands-on project
* Collaborate with a community of builders, researchers, and innovators across the African AI ecosystem
### Who it's for
 
Students, early-career professionals, aspiring AI engineers, researchers, developers, and anyone working to transition into AI research. The curriculum is designed for learners with a technical background in mathematics, computer science, physics, or engineering. No prior machine-learning experience is required.
