## What you'll learn

This foundational course introduces artificial intelligence from first principles. By the end of the eight weeks, you'll have a working mental model of how modern AI systems are built, trained, and deployed - and you'll have built one yourself.

## Topics

- The mathematics behind machine learning - just enough linear algebra, probability, and calculus to follow what's happening
- Supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning at a conceptual level
- Building and training neural networks with practical tooling
- The transformer architecture and how it underpins modern language models
- Responsible AI: bias, evaluation, and deployment considerations

## Format

Weekly live sessions, asynchronous lectures, hands-on coding labs, and a capstone project completed in pairs.

## Prerequisites

Comfort with Python at an intermediate level. No prior machine-learning experience required.
