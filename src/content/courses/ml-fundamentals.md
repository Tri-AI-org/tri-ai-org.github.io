## What you'll learn

A grounding in classical machine learning before you reach for deep networks. The course is structured around the core families of ML methods and the evaluation discipline that separates working models from misleading ones.

## Topics

- Linear and logistic regression, decision trees, random forests
- Clustering and dimensionality reduction
- Cross-validation, train/test discipline, and metric selection
- Feature engineering and data preparation
- A first look at when classical methods beat deep learning - and when they don't

## Format

Six weeks of weekly modules, each anchored by a coding notebook and a small dataset to work through.
