## What you'll learn

This course takes you from the basics of neural networks through the transformer architectures that power today's language and vision models. Emphasis throughout is on building intuition through implementation — you'll write your own networks, train them, and debug what goes wrong.

## Topics

- Forward and backward propagation from scratch
- Convolutional networks and modern computer-vision architectures
- Recurrent networks and the path from RNNs to attention
- The transformer architecture in depth
- Training dynamics, optimisation, and common failure modes

## Prerequisites

The AI Foundations or Machine Learning Fundamentals course (or equivalent prior experience). Comfort with Python and basic linear algebra.
