## About the programme

TRI AI Saturdays Lagos is the flagship teaching initiative of TRI AI — a community-led AI study group that has run multiple cohorts of learners through structured curricula since its founding. Each cohort spans roughly twelve weeks and combines self-paced study with weekly meet-ups, mentorship from working AI practitioners, and peer-learning sessions.

## What participants do

- Work through curated AI/ML curricula at their own pace
- Meet weekly in-person or online for discussion, doubt-clearing, and project work
- Pair with mentors drawn from industry and research
- Build a capstone project presented at cohort end

## Who it's for

The programme is open to learners across Nigeria and the wider African continent who have basic programming experience and want to build a foundation in AI. We particularly welcome applicants from groups under-represented in AI.

## Outcomes

Past cohorts have produced graduates working at AI labs, research groups, and product teams across Africa and globally. Many alumni return as mentors for subsequent cohorts.
