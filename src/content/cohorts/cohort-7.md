Cohort 7 was structured around a parallel track: 16 weeks of weekly classes covering theory, with 11 weeks of practicals layered alongside for applied work. Students were grouped into five teams, each named after an African leader.

## Capstone teams

| Team | Project |
| --- | --- |
| Johnson-Sirleaf | REALICE |
| Kaunda | Rent prediction in Nigeria |
| Machel | Easy Buy recommendation system |
| Sankara | iRecommend |
| Selassie | Nigerian food price forecasting |

Project proposals and team structures are available in the [cohort 7 projects repository](https://github.com/AISaturdaysLagos/cohort7_projects).
