TRI AI is an NGO committed to revolutionising the teaching, research and innovation in artificial intelligence in Africa.

Registered as: Artificial Intelligence Teaching Research and Innovation for Africa Ltd/Gte

Contact Us Learn More

Our Mission

To democratize artificial intelligence by creating African artificial intelligence communities that enable and facilitate the studying, researching, and building of artificial intelligence products for our immediate community and beyond.

Our Vision

To be at the forefront of artificial intelligence research and development in Africa and for Africa.

AI Saturdays Lagos

Founded in 2018, AI Saturdays Lagos offers free AI and machine learning classes to bridge the skill gap in Africa. Join us to explore data science, deep learning, and research.
